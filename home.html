About Timazi Africa

Timazi Africa is a non-denominational ministry that proclaims the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the High school students in Africa to know Him, love Him, and live for Him. We further seek to resource those who minister to the High School student (Educator/Youth Pastor/Parent) through training and publishing discipleship resources.
